double  rotation,
boolean  is_quick_turn

Wrapper around 254's drive interface.

The "turning" stick controls the curvature of the robot's path rather than its rate of heading change. This helps make the robot more controllable at high speeds. Also handles the robot's quick turn functionality - "quick turn" overrides constant-curvature turning for turn-in-place maneuvers.

Parameters
speedForward speed (from -1.0 to 1.0)
rotationRotation of robot (from -1.0 to 1.0)
is_quick_turnIs quick turn functionality enabled?
